About Satalitor

Satalitor is an advanced platform that allows users to explore and analyze specific regions of Earth using satellite imagery. By selecting an area on the map, our system performs object detection to provide insights about land use, vegetation, urban development, and more.

Space Analysis

See the different type of land by using space satalite from ur house

Urban Development

Our system provides insights about urban development in a specific area, including the density of buildings, roads, and more.

Land Use Analysis

Our system provides detailed information about the land use of a specific area, including vegetation, urban development, and more.
